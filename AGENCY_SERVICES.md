# 🏢 Datalazo Intelligence Agency: Services Portfolio

This document outlines the four core pillars of Datalazo. It serves as both an internal guide and a sales blueprint for clients.

---

## 1. 🎙️ AI Voice Agents
**Value**: 24/7 human-like voice communication for lead qualification and support.
-   **Tech**: MediaRecorder (Browser) + OpenAI Whisper (STT) + GPT-4o-mini (LLM) + TTS-1 (Voice).
-   **Key Feature**: "Fast Mode" unified processing for < 3s latency.
-   **Use Case**: Real estate booking, dental appointment reminders, customer support lines.

## 2. 💬 AI Customer Service
**Value**: Automate 90% of customer queries with intelligent, context-aware chat agents.
-   **Tech**: Next.js + OpenAI Chat API + RAG (Knowledge Base).
-   **Key Feature**: Trained on specific company data (PDFs, URLs, Docs) to never give a wrong answer.
-   **Use Case**: SaaS support, e-commerce product recommendations, FAQ automation.

## 3. ⚙️ Process Automation
**Value**: Connect disconnected tools and automate repetitive tasks.
-   **Tech**: n8n (Self-hosted) + API Integrations.
-   **Key Feature**: Custom workflows that "glue" your CRM, Email, and internal tools together.
-   **Use Case**: Automatic lead entry into CRM from emails, automated invoicing, social media posting.

## 4. 🚀 AI SEO Matrix
**Value**: Dominate Google search results with AI-optimized content and technical strategy.
-   **Tech**: Programmatic SEO + AI Content Agents.
-   **Key Feature**: Scale from 10 to 1,000 high-quality landing pages automatically.
-   **Use Case**: Local SEO for franchises, blog content factories, automated competitor analysis.
